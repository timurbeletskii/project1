My part time business on eBay

In my free time, I run a small reselling business on eBay. Essentially, this involves buying clothing and shoes from discount brick and mortar stores like Marshalls, Burlington Coat Factory, Nordstrom Rack, etcera, and reselling them on eBay for a markup.

This isn't a scam or anything, these brick and mortar stores just sell these shoes at under market price. For example, I recently bought a pair of Nike Vapormax shoes for about 30 dollars in Nordstrom Rack. These shoes retail for 220 dollars in stores like Foot Locker and Champs, and sell for similar amounts on websites like eBay or Facebook Marketplace. I was able to sell them for 200 dollars

This is my seller page, as you can see I have all positive reviews.
